Login and Registration
  • How do I create an account?

    • To create an account, click on the 'Register' button on the top right of the homepage, fill in your details, and submit.

  • I forgot my password, how do I reset it?

    • Click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

  • How do I verify my email address?

    • After registering, you'll receive a verification email. Click on the link in the email to verify your address.

Voting and Competitions
  • How do I vote in a competition?

    • To vote, navigate to the 'Current Events' section, select an event, and click 'Vote'.

  • Can I apply to create my own competition?

    • Yes, click on 'Create Competition' under your profile dashboard, fill out the required information, and submit for approval.

  • How do I delete a competition I created?

    • To delete a competition, go to your profile dashboard, find the competition, and click 'Delete'. Only competitions without active votes can be deleted.

Donations
  • How do I make a donation?

    • Click on the 'Donate' button available on the homepage or the competition pages, select an amount, and proceed with payment.

  • Is my donation tax-deductible?

    • All donations are tax-deductible. You will receive a confirmation email with details for your records.

Profile Management
  • How do I update my profile information?

    • Go to your profile page and click 'Edit Profile'. Update your details and save changes.

  • How can I change my profile picture?

    • Click on your current profile picture, upload a new one, and save changes.

General Help
  • How do I contact support?

    • You can send a message to our support team through the 'Support' section in the Help Center.

  • What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

    • If you encounter a technical issue, please report it through the 'Support' section or email us directly at support@makeachange.com.
